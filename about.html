    About Us

    Our Mission Statement

    Student Information Technology Services (SITS) is a student-led organization providing educational opportunities to students in information technology, networking, and cybersecurity. We supply no-cost computers to students who lack access to such technology at home.

    We serve Southeastern Michigan on a mission to give young people valuable work-based learning experience while helping to reduce the digital divide.

    Once we receive your hardware donations, our student volunteers will handle reconditioning from beginning to end. Students inventory all donations and delegate responsibilities to administrators who then lead a group of technicians through the refurbishing process. All equipment is cleaned, tested, and upgraded by our student volunteers, and we image open-source operating systems to each machine. Installations are completed with custom educational software packages and machines are tested again before distribution. These systems make their ways into the homes of children without computers.

    Our organization is largely student-led, allowing student volunteers to gain experience serving in roles such as administrators and technicians, with some holding seats on the board. We are constantly seeking donations of unused or decommissioned computers, components, and peripherals so that we can bring them back to life and pass them on to students without access to these devices in their household.
