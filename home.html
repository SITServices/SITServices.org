    Do you have any unused computers, components, or peripherals lying around your house? Got decommissioned PCs you are looking to get rid of? Consider donating to Student Information Technology Services by dropping off unused technology in Canton room CV 26. Student Information Technology Services (SITS) is an organization that takes old tech, makes it new again, and donates refurbished computers to other organizations or households that don't have access to such technology. Donations can be made between 6:30AM to 3:00PM. For more information, please contact us at studentinfotechservices@gmail.com, or visit our about us page. No donation is too small.

    What Do We Do?

    • We take in and refurbish old hardware.
    • We educate customers and students.
    • We donate our refurbished tech to local companies and foundations that need them.
