    Team Bright Pigs Place 2nd in Michigan Governor's High School Cyber Challenge

    Team Bright Pigs of the SITS group recently took home the 2nd place trophy during the 2019 Michigan Governor's High School Cyber Challenge. The challenge tests student's knowlege in cybersecurity and related topics. Teams of up to three high school students compete in a virtual Capture-the-Flag activity designed to test their skills and abilities. For more information, visit the official website.
